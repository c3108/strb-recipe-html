strawberry red velvet cake

delicate and airy chiffon cake infused with a rich cocoa red velvet flavor. generously filled with whipped cream cheese and tangy strawberries that complement the soft texture of the chiffon.

ingredients

6 inch mold

  • 3 eggs
  • 40g cake flour
  • 25g corn oil
  • 50g milk
  • 5g cocoa powder
  • 6g strawberry powder
  • 6g lemon juice
  • 30g fine sugar
  • 1g salt

toppings

  • whipped cream cheese
  • strawberry
  • biscuit

steps

  1. whisk corn oil, strawberry powder, and cocoa powder.
  2. pour in milk, whisk until emulsified.
  3. sift in cake flour, whisk until smooth.
  4. add egg yolks, whisk until smooth.
  5. in a separate bowl, add sugar to cold egg whites. beat until soft peaks.
  6. fold the meringue into the batter in 2 parts.
  7. transfer into molds, tap out air bubbles.
  8. bake 120°C in oven for 70 minutes.
  9. let fully cool, decorate with whipped cream cheese, strawberries, and biscuits.
