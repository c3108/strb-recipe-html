strawberry cupcake

a light and fluffy cupcake with a chiffon cake base and bursting with fresh strawberry flavor. the airy sponge, infused with real strawberry purée, pairs perfectly with each other. a delicate and refreshing treat.

ingredients

chiffon

  • 48g cake flour
  • 40g milk
  • 30g granulated sugar
  • 25g corn oil
  • 3 eggs

toppings

  • whipped cream cheese
  • strawberry

steps

  1. separate the yolks of the eggs.
  2. mix sugar with egg yolks.
  3. pour in milk and corn oil, stir until emulsified.
  4. sift in the cake flour, whisk until smooth.
  5. in a separate bowl, whisk cold egg whites until stiff peaks to make a meringue.
  6. fold egg whites into the batter, careful not to overmix.
  7. pour into molds, 80-90% full.
  8. preheat oven to 125°C, bake for 25 minutes.
  9. raise temperature to 145°C, bake for 20 minutes.
