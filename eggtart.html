strawberry egg tart

rich and velvety homemade egg tarts with a buttery, flaky pastry crust and silky smooth custard filling. topped with airy whipped cream, slightly sweet strawberry jam, and three different kinds of strawberries to balance the sweetness.

ingredients

crust

  • 195g cake flour
  • 135g cold unsalted butter
  • 45ml cold water
  • 5g sugar
  • 1g salt

filling

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 70g sugar
  • 10g corn starch
  • 200ml whipping cream
  • 100ml milk
  • a dash of vanilla bean paste

toppings

  • whipped cream
  • white strawberry
  • light red strawberry
  • dark red strawberry

steps

  1. chop butter into cubes, use a scraper and mix it with cake flour.
  2. pour in water, knead with hands. refrigerate for 1h.
  3. whisk 3 egg yolks with sugar until emulsified.
  4. add cornstarch, continue whisking until fluffy.
  5. mix whipping cream, milk, and vanilla bean paste. put in microwave for 1min 30s.
  6. while whisking, pour the liquid into the egg yolks. strain and refrigerate.
  7. roll out crust dough, fold, and roll again until rectangular. refrigerate for 1h.
  8. sprinkle cake flour on crust dough, roll until thin, and cut into circles to fit in muffin pan.
  9. press dough into pan, use a fork and lightly press the dough.
  10. pour custard filling inside, bake 190°C in oven.
  11. pipe whipped cream around the rim, and put a spoonful of strawberry jam inside.
  12. continue piping whipped cream until it reaches the top, decorate with assorted strawberries.
