strawberry cheesecake

the perfect blend of creamy and fruity in one cake. featuring a buttery crust and a velvety layer of rich cheesecake infused with the tartness of strawberries.

ingredients

cheesecake

  • 30g butter
  • 60g crushed biscuits
  • 30g granulated sugar
  • 100g cream cheese
  • 5g gelatin sheets
  • 25g milk
  • 80ml whipping cream
  • 10g strawberry jam
  • 5g strawberry powder

toppings

  • whipped cream cheese
  • strawberry

steps

  1. melt butter, let cool.
  2. pour butter into crushed biscuits.
  3. flatten into the bottom of each mold.
  4. in a separate bowl, add sugar into cream cheese, mix well with a spoon.
  5. put softened gelatin sheets into hot milk, let it melt.
  6. pour the milk into the cream cheese, mix well.
  7. add whipping cream, strawberry jam and strawberry powder, mix well.
  8. pour into molds, refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  9. release from molds and decorate with whipped cream cheese and strawberries.
